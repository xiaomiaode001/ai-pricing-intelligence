# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Contributors are expected to communicate respectfully, assume good intent, and keep discussions focused on improving the project.

Examples of helpful behavior:

- explain tradeoffs clearly
- keep feedback specific and actionable
- respect source-policy and data-accuracy boundaries
- avoid publishing private data, credentials, or personal payment details

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, close, or remove issues, pull requests, or comments that are abusive, off-topic, or unsafe. Repeated violations may result in blocked participation.
